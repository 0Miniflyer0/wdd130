Top video game picks of 2023!

It is nearing the end of the year: 2023. With 2023 there came a whole load of memories. But not just memories, but also Video Games! There have been a lot of video games that were released this year. You're probably reading this and wondering: "Why am i writing this page when I can go and play these games myself?.

Playing video games are a huge hobby of mine. And now in the year 2023, I believe that a lot of triple A gaming companes, care more about money than they do of making actual functioning and fun games. I don't want people to buy a game based on hype and popular opinion, I want people to actually play a game that they will enjoy it. Rather than buy a game, based on hype, and ending up wasting like 60 bucks! I'm going to write about 2 video games which i personally played, these truly stood out to me this year. Ones that really, REALLY blew my mind. And I truly enjoyed the moments when I played them.
