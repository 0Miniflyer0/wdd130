Rivers: Main Fork Of Salmon River: Idaho
Nestled within the Frank Church Wilderness Of No Return Winds the Salmon River. The samon was given the nickname long ago as the River of No Reutnr by people living it. Because of the number of folks that had come to visit and fell so in love with it's sheer beauty. Of the river that they never really left.
And of course... The Rapids. They range from Class I to IV The Salmon river rapids is one the longest undammned rivers in the States. As a free flowing river the water is higher, faster and colder in the early summer, and lower, slower and warmer in the late summer. There are good rapids all summer. But families with children who like to swim and appreciate late summer's warm waters. Should definetly come here and take a visit.
